Program

The detailed program for GraphSys 2026 will be published once the workshop schedule is finalized (by July 24, 2026).

GraphSys 2026 will be co-located with EuroPar 2026 in Pisa, Italy. Workshop dates: August 24–25, 2026.

The workshop will run a single track with one keynote, paper presentations, flash presentations and posters for the three challenges (causal discovery in HPC and datacenter management; causal understanding of quantum uncertainty; causal hardware–software co-design), and one panel.
